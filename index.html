About

KLE's College of BCA, RLS Institute proudly presents "Rhythm" - A Celebration of Cultural Harmony! Get ready to immerse yourself in a world of music, dance, art, and culture! Our festival has something for everyone: Ranbhoomi : Engage in thrilling games and competitions that will test your skills and strategies. Vichaar Sangram: Showcase your debating skills and defend what's right in our battle of wits. RANGOTSAV: Unleash your creativity and explore your artistic side through our various art competitions. Sur Kshetra : Sing your heart out and compete for the coveted title of Nightbird. Chakravyuah: Embark on a treasure hunt and discover the hidden secrets of our festival. Parishit : Put your knowledge to the test and participate in our quizzes and trivia competitions. Sanskriti Samrat: Mesmerize the audience with your confidence and enthusiasm and compete for the top spot. Taal Tarang: Get ready to groove to the rhythm and show off your best dance moves! Milan Manch: This unites an experience of innovation of great ideas and performances. This is the event you don’t want to miss. Chitramaya : a photography event that weaves the rich tapestry of Indian culture through the lens of artistry. Join us for an unforgettable experience! Mark your calendars and get ready to be a part of "RHYTHM”
