GENERAL RULES



GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS


  • 1. Registration is compulsory for each event.
    2. One student can participate in only one event (except Ice breaker).
    3. Ice breaker team members must also be a participant of atleast 1 other event during the Rhythm fest.
    4. On spot registration is allowed for solo and pair events.
    5. Participants are requested to carry lunch and water bottles during the event ,as per their requirement.
    6. Students taking part in the event should confirm their registration on the day of the fest at the registration counter.
    7. Smoking, drinking and consumption of any such 'substances' are prohibited. Anything that can be perceived as being offensive to any national and/or religious sentiments, gender, caste, race etc. will lead to immediate disqualification.
    8. The offline registration will start sharp at 8:30 AM and ends at 9:30 AM.
    9. The judges decision will be final.
    10. Last day for Team event registration is 9th December 2024.
    11. For securing the General Championship participating in the following events is mandatory: Photography/Videography, Debate, Quiz, Treasure Hunt, Master & Miss, Ice Breaker.
    12. Winners will be selected based on their points achieved from round 1 itself.
    13. Debate , dance, singing and Ice breaker participants can go directly to KLE centenary convention center Dr.B.S Jirge Auditorium,JNMC Campus ,Belagavi for their respective events.
    14. The valedictory function will be in KLE centenary convention center Dr.B.S Jirge Auditorium,JNMC Campus ,Belagavi on 13 dec from 3:30pm onward.
