RANGOUTSAV

(Painting)

Are you ready to bring your imagination to life? Join us for a vibrant competition where art meets creativity!
Whether it’s painting the beauty of diversity or designing a logo that captures the spirit of our nation, this is your chance to showcase your talent. Let your brush or digital tools celebrate the rich traditions, vibrant festivals, and unique stories of our culture. From vivid paintings to iconic logo designs, explore the essence of India and beyond through your artistic vision.It’s not just a competition – it’s an inspiring journey where colors, shapes, and ideas come together to tell a story. Let your creativity shine bright and leave your mark in this celebration of art!



RULES AND REGULATIONS


  1. Number of participants 2 per team.


  2. Participants must follow all the themes and additional instructions provided for each round.

  3. Materials to be brought by participants :- Paints (only acrylic, water colors, poster colors are allowed, no crayons).
    • Brushes, pencils (2B, 4B, 8B etc.), sketch pens, and other requirements according to your choice.


    4. NOTE Paper or canvas will be provided.
