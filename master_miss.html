CHAKRAVYUHA

(Treasure hunt)

Chakravyuha : A Treasure Hunt Adventure! Embark on an exciting journey to uncover hidden treasures! In this treasure hunt, participants will follow a series of clues scattered across the area, solve fun riddles, and complete challenges. Whether you're racing against the clock or working as a team, the hunt leads to the discovery of a hidden prize



RULES AND REGULATIONS


  1. Number of participants per team :04.

  2. The treasure hunt consists of various Rounds, each with increasing levels of difficulty.

  3. Teams will face a variety of challenges, and only the top performing teams in each round will advance to the next change.

  4. Participants will be tested with a variety of questions, including: Maths Riddles, Tricky Riddles and Situation Based Questions.

  5. The use of Smartphones is strictly prohibited unless explicitly permitted by the organizers.
